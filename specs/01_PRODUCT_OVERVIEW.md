## 1. Product Vision
This project is a beauty business directory platform combining:
- Business listing (directory)
- Membership system
- Custom landing page builder for beauty businesses

The platform targets spas, salons, clinics, dental clinics, nail studios, and beauty brands.

## 2. Core Goals
- Help users find trusted beauty businesses
- Help businesses build a high-conversion landing page without coding
- Monetize via membership packages (Free / Premium / VIP)

## 3. User Types
- Guest: browse directory, search, view landing pages
- Business Member: manage profile, landing page, media, services
- Admin: manage system, users, payments, content

## 4. Key Principles
- SEO-first
- Mobile-first
- High conversion (CTA, booking, contact)
- Scalable architecture

## 5. MVP Scope
- Directory + Search
- Business registration
- Membership & manual payment approval
- Business landing pages
- Admin panel
